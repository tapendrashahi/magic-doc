              Aerobic respiration has four continuous processes:

              • Glycolysis in cytosol/cytoplasm (EMP pathway),
              • Oxidative decarboxylation in outer and Perimitochondrial space,
              • Kreb cycle in mitochondrial matrix (Tricarboxylic acid cycle or TCA cycle) but in bacteria it occurs in cytosol and
              • Oxidative phosphorylation in inner membrane of mitochondria (Terminal oxidation, ETS)
