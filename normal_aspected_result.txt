<h1>Limitations of Statistics</h1>

<p><strong>Although useful, statistics also has some limitations.</strong></p>

<div> </div>

<h2>What are the Limitations of Statistics?</h2>

<div> 
  <ol>
    <li> <strong>Deals Only with Quantitative Data:</strong> 
<p>It cannot measure feelings or qualities.</p>
 
<p>Example: Can&apos;t measure happiness of people.</p>
 </li>
    <li> <strong>Possibility of Misuse:</strong> 
<p>Data can be misused to show false results or bias.</p>
 </li>
    <li> <strong>Does not show exact truth:</strong> 
<p>Statistics shows averages, not the exact situation of individuals.</p>
 </li>
    <li> <strong>Needs Skilled Persons:</strong> 
<p>Proper knowledge is needed for data collection and interpretation.</p>
 </li>
    <li> <strong>Depends on Data Accuracy:</strong> 
<p>Wrong or incomplete data leads to wrong conclusions.</p>
 </li>
  </ol>
 </div>
