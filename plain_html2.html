Ellipse

An ellipse is the locus of the point $\mathrm{P}(\mathrm{x}, \mathrm{y})$ in a plane such that sum of its distances from two fixed points is always constant.

A conic section having eccentricity less than one is called ellipse ( $\mathrm{e}<1$ The ratio of the distance of any point on ellipse from the fixed point to its distance from a fixed line is always constant which is less than 1 . $$ \frac{S P}{P M}=\text { constant }=e $$
